Services

Woojer Vest 3 Rental Programs

We are excited to offer the rental of 50 high-end Woojer Vest 3 haptic vests. These vests deliver spatial haptics, allowing users to feel vibrations from all directions. This technology is particularly beneficial for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing (DHH) community, as it can separate complex music into left and right channels, enabling the appreciation of musical elements without it turning into white noise. Our vests are ready to enhance your events with a truly immersive experience!

Accessibility Consulting

Our diverse team offers consulting services to help you make your events and spaces more accessible. One notable project is our live captioning system for the Woodstock Cafe, also known as the Sign Language Cafe in Portland. This system enables hearing non-signers to communicate effortlessly with their deaf colleagues by capturing speech directed at a microphone and displaying real-time captions. We provide solutions that foster inclusivity and effective communication.

DJ Services

Our talented member, known as DeafWish, is an experienced deaf DJ who has successfully performed at numerous events. DeafWish brings a unique perspective and energy to each performance, creating an inclusive and dynamic atmosphere for all attendees.

Workshops

We offer workshops led by a highly skilled engineer and educator. These sessions guide participants through the process of creating their first audio-reactive device from scratch using affordable and accessible technology. Our workshops are a fantastic introduction to programming and hardware integration, perfect for beginners and enthusiasts alike.
